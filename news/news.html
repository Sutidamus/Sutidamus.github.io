Diamond Takes First at Our 21st Smash for Wii U Tournament

It seems that despite public opinion of the character, Luigi still remains atop the top 20 spots on Gamer Haven's Super Smash Bros. for Wii U Tier list of Diamond took 1st place at our 21st tournament for the game this past Friday. In a close match between the tournament's second best player, Anon, Diamond defeated the Lucario main with a simple down throw into up-special combo. While many spectators were quite infuriated with the outcome, the only thing Diamond had to say after the match was, "I do what I have to do to win. I want to win."

Xenoblade Chronicles is Back in Stock

The classic Wii Japanese RPG, Xenoblade Chronicles, is officially back in stock. While the games hasn't exactly been made extremely available to say the least, Nintendo is taking steps to promote and restock the game as quickly as possible. Xenoblade Chronicles released in 2011 and is regarded as one of the best RPGs of all time. It was Gamer Haven's 2011 Game of the Year winner and is a favorite among the staff. Your motivation to dust off you Wii is finally here...again. (Buy now)

Persona 5 Takes America by Storm

Whereas previous entries in the Persona franchise have not exactly succeeded in engaging new fans and players in the west, Persona 5 has successfully won over the previously thought intractable market. The new addition to the blockbuster Japanese RPG series has sold over 1 million units in its first week on the market! Congratulations ATLUS for turning a gaming masterpiece into a world wide icon for the medium.
